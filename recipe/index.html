Grandma’s Secret Chocolate Chip Cookies

A timeless classic - crisp on the edges, chewy in the centre.

Ingredients

  • 200 g wheat flour or spelt flour
  • 90 g sugar
  • 125 g margerine
  • 8 g vanilla sugar
  • 5 g baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons apple sauce
  • 150 g chocolate chips
  • ½ tbsp salt

Method

  1. Pre‑heat oven to 180 °C (350 °F). Line two baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. Mix all of the ingredients except chocolate chips in a large bowl.
  3. Mix in the chocolate chips.
  4. Form 18 balls (about 40g each) out of the dough and place on baking trays.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes.
  6. The cookies should still be relatively soft. Flatten them with a spatula if you want to.
  7. Let them cool for 10 minutes before carefully removing them from the trays.
